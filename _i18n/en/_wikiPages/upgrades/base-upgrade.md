### Crafting

It is crafted using the [HT Crafting Table]({{ "/wiki/ht-crafting-table" | relative_url }}):  

- Redstone x 4  
- Nether Quartz x 2  
- Gold Ignot x 2  
- Glass x 1  
- [Star Dust]({{ "wiki/star-dust.html" | relative_url }}) x 5
