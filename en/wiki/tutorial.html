How to join Galaxy

On the Space Ship Click Space Ship Controller on the table
Switch to All galaxy using the button on the top-right corner

Find Galaxy that you want to join

Click Join request

Wait for Admin or Owner to Approve your request
Or you can ask Admin or Owner to add you manually

Maybe you can create your own one！

How to enter Planet

On the Space Ship Click Space Ship Controller on the table
Switch to All galaxy or Joined galaxy using the button on the top-right corner

Click Galaxy that you want to enter

Click Planet List

Click Planet that you want to enter

How to enter other world

On the Planet Click Planet Terminal
Click Planet List

Click Planet that you want to enter

How to back to Space Ship

On the Planet Click Planet Terminal
Click Back to SpaceShip
